After a few weeks of building, The Free State of Ligma's first building site has been completed!

Written by President Aaron Innecco on the 6th of June 2025.

In a historic moment for The Free State of Ligma, the first official construction project has been completed, a brand-new road, marking a significant step toward development and connectivity within the nation. Once a mere construction site, this newly paved route now stands as a symbol of progress.

The construction site started out as a huge field with a rocky side on one side, and tall grass on the other, this is where the first base was created, and the idea of "taking over" the area was founded. 5 years after the construction site became a thing, they then began to build, destroying the first base. It took a while, but eventually, houses 38A, 38B, 38C and 38D were created (keep in mind that this was before the Free State of Ligma was a thing). After that, no one went into the construction site for a while, until we started back up again, with the MANCATS Movement.
